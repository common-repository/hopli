Make your CSR actions the ones of your clients

== Description ==

At Hopli we allow you to donate a small amount of your revenue after each successful purchase, but with one big difference: your client choose the association !

All you have to do is:
* Choose an amount to give
* Pre select up to 6 associations that you want to support
* Let your client choose and co-create your CSR actions

Why go with Hopli ?

It is easy to install: less than one minute ! And we handle the rest !

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How can I obtain my client and app ID ? =

For now, you need to contact [contact@hopli.io](mailto:contact@hopli.io) or go to [https://hopli.io/#demo-calendly](https://hopli.io/#demo-calendly) and ask for a demo.

== Installation ==
1. Search 'Hopli' in the Plugins/Add New
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Configure plugin (go to the Settings/Hopli)
